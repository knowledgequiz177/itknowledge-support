Delete Your Account

Last updated: June 18, 2026

If you would like to delete your account and remove your data from IT Knowledge, follow these steps:

  1. Go to your Profile

    Open the app and tap your profile icon.

  2. Select Delete Account

    Scroll to the bottom of your profile settings and tap Delete Account.

  3. Confirm Deletion

    Confirm the action. This action may be permanent and cannot be undone.
