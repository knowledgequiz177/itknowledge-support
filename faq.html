Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about IT Knowledge.

IT Knowledge is an educational quiz app designed to help users improve their information technology knowledge through quizzes and training activities.

Open the app, sign in, choose your quiz topic or model, then start the quiz from the home screen.

You can create an account directly inside the app using your email address.

Use the Forgot Password option on the login screen and follow the instructions sent to your email.

Credits can be purchased directly inside the app using the available credit packages.

Please restart the app first. If the issue continues, contact us with your account email and purchase details.

Send a detailed description of the issue to knowledgequiz177@gmail.com. Screenshots are helpful.

Some features, including login, purchases, and account services, may require an internet connection.
